About me

I've always loved photography, ever since I was a little child and got my hands on my first Polaroid camera. The world somehow makes more sense when observed through a lens, and I find that by finding and looking for new perspectives, you understand certain aspects of life more thoroughly. In my photography, I always try to capture the essence of the moment, and communicate how I experience and feel.
